About Me

I'm a 2nd year Computer Engineering student who believes technology should enhance human capabilities, not diminish them. My approach is simple: I'm not just a "learner" — I'm a solver.

I dive deep into concepts, asking "why?" until I reach the core understanding. My current focus lies in Deep Learning, RAG systems, and LLM applications — building practical AI solutions that solve real problems.

Looking ahead, I'm fascinated by the intersection of Quantum Computing, Neuroscience, and Artificial Intelligence. My long-term vision is to understand how neurons work at a fundamental level and apply those insights to create more human-aligned AI systems.
